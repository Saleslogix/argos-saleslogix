Using Contour

The Contour features enable searching for Accounts that are in a specific geographical proximity. Results are limited to a 500 mile radius.

  • Search for accounts from a specific starting point.

  • Search for nearby accounts using an account as your starting point.

Finding Contour features

  • From the Global menu:

    • Tap Accounts Near Me to search for Accounts using the current GPS coordinates of your device. Use the Account Type drop-down to filter the results by Suspect, Lead, Prospect, Customer, Partner, Vendor, Influencer, Competitor, and Other. Search results display in the Account Detail view.

    • Tap Contour Distance Search to find Accounts in proximity to specific locations such as a local business or landmark. These locations are set up in Infor CRM in Places and include places shared by other users. If your Infor CRM user profile contains office and home information, My Office and My House will also be listed as options. Use the Lookup field to enter an address or choose from the available list.

  • From an Account record Detail view:

    • In the Detail view header bar, tap View Accounts Nearby to search starting at the selected account.
