Infor CRM SLX Back Office Extension (ICBOE) adds the following entities to the Mobile Client:
  • Invoices
  • Quotes
  • Sales Orders
  • Shipments
  • Receivables
To see these entities, you must enable the views:
  • Tap Global Menu> Other > Configure Menu, tap Invoices, Receivables, and/or Shipments. In the top menu bar, tap the check mark icon to accept the selections and exit the Configure Menu.
  • If necessary, set a default group for an entity: In the entity list view, tap No default group set. Tap here to configure groups and select to see either all available records for that entity or just your records for that entity.

  • The entity detail view Related Items tabs provide access to additional information. Open a detail view for the following entity types to access the items in the Related Items tab:

    • Invoices: Invoice Items and Receivable Items.
    • Quotes: Quote Lines, Attachments, Sales Persons and Sync History.
    • Receivables: Receivable Items
    • Sales Orders: Order Lines, Invoice Lines, Shipment Lines, Attachments, and Sync History
    • Shipments: Shipment lines

Quick Tips

  • Groups are available for Quotes and Sales Orders.
  • The Delete button is available on the Quote and Order line items, tap it to delete the current record from CRM. This is available only on the detail view of the line item.
  • The Working offline Recently Viewed and My Briefcase features are both available for ICBOE records.

Dashboards

Use Dashboards to see Account-related customizable, pre-defined analytics in the form of list widgets.

On the Account detail layout, tap the Dashboards tab.

  • Financial Snapshot: Displays information over a configurable 30, 60, 90, 180, and 365 day period.
    • Invoiced Revenue: Total Extended Amount for invoices related to the account created in the last x days.
    • Invoiced Profit: (Total Extended Amount) less the (Total Cost) for invoices related to the account created in the last x days.
    • Invoiced Margin: (Invoiced Profit Amount) divided by the (Extended Amount) for invoices related to the account created in the last x days.
    • YoY Revenue: (Revenue for the past year less the Revenue between 2 years and 1 year prior) divided by the (Revenue between 2 years and 1 year prior).
    • YoY Profit: (Total Extended Amount) less the (Total Cost) for invoices related to the account created in the last x days.
    • YoY Margin: (Margin for the past year less the Margin between 2 years and 1 year prior) divided by the (Margin between 2 years and 1 years prior) for invoice related to the account created in the last x days.
  • New Transactions: Displays over a configurable 7, 14, 30, and 90 day period that includes the current date. It displays both the number of invoices and their total value.
    • New Quotes: The number of quotes related to the account created in the last x days.
    • New Orders: The number of orders related to the account created in the last x days.
    • New Shipments: The number of shipments related to this account created in the last x days.
    • New Invoices: The number of invoices related to the account created in the last x days.
  • Open Transactions: Displays open Aging Quotes, Aging Invoices, and Aging Sales Orders over a configurable 0+, 30+, 60+, 90+, and 180+ day period prior to the current date. It displays both the number of transaction records and their total value.
    • Aging Quotes: The number of total open quotes related to the account created in the last x days.
    • Aging Invoices: The number of total open invoices related to the account created in the last x days.
    • Aging Sales Orders: The number of total open sales orders related to this account created in the last x days.
